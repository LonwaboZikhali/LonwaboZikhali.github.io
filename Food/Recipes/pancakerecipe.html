Golden-Brown Banana Pancakes

Fluffy, naturally sweet pancakes made with ripe bananas, offering a hint of caramel flavor as they cook to a golden-brown perfection. Ideal for breakfast or brunch, theyâ€™re easy to make, require just a few ingredients, and are a healthy, delicious twist on classic pancakes.

Preparation: 60 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoon melted butter
  • 1.25 cup milk

Step 1

Crush the banana until it is well mashed.

Sift dry ingredients together in a bowl.

Mix butter, milk, egg and banana.

Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients, then mix until smooth.

Step 2

Heat some oil in a pan on medium heat.

Pour a scoop of batter into the pan, depending on the size you desire.

Allow it to cook until edges are dry and bubbles start to form (~2 minutes), then flip and cook until browned on both sides.

Repeat with remaining batter.

Step 3

Enjoy! (Garnish!!!!!)
